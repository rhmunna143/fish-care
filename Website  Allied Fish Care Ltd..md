🌐 **Website Structure for Allied Fish Care Ltd.**

**1. Home Page**

**Goal:** Make a strong first impression.\
**Content:**

- Hero banner with company name, tagline, and image of fish farms or ponds.\
  *Example tagline:* “Sustainable Solutions for a Healthier Aquaculture Future.”
- Short intro: Who you are and what you do.
- Highlights of key products/services.
- Contact or “Get in Touch” button.
-----
**2. About Us**

**Goal:** Build trust and share your story.\
**Content:**

- Company overview (like the “About” I wrote earlier).
- Mission, vision, and values.
- Short timeline or story of how the company started.
- Photos of your team, facilities, or projects.
-----
**3. Products & Services**

**Goal:** Show what you offer.\
**Sections:**

- Fish Feed
- Water Treatment Solutions
- Fish Health & Probiotics
- Consultancy & Farm Support Services\
  Each with images, short descriptions, and benefits.
-----
**4. Gallery / Projects**

**Goal:** Show real impact and professionalism.\
**Content:**

- Photos of farms, ponds, packaging, or customers.
- Optional: before/after results or testimonials from fish farmers.
-----
**5. Blog / News (Optional)**

**Goal:** Establish authority and SEO.\
**Content ideas:**

- “5 Tips for Maintaining Water Quality in Fish Ponds”
- “The Role of Probiotics in Fish Health”
- Company announcements or events.
-----
**6. Contact Us**

**Goal:** Make it easy for customers to reach you.\
**Content:**

- Address, phone, and email.
- Contact form.
- Google Map embed.
- Links to social media (Facebook, WhatsApp, LinkedIn, etc.).
-----
**7. Footer**

**Content:**

- Quick links to main pages.
- Copyright © Allied Fish Care Ltd. [Year].
- “All rights reserved.”
- Social media icons.
-----
**💡 Design Suggestions**

- **Theme:** Blue & aqua tones (symbolize water and trust).
- **Style:** Clean, modern, with rounded sections and fish/aquatic graphics.
